PersinHub, will run on phub.ch

1. Business Model — How does this make money?

The platform will initially focus on building trust, content, and a strong user base by offering free business listings.

In later stages, a freemium model will be introduced:

Free listings remain available for all businesses
Paid “featured” options will offer increased visibility (e.g., top placement, highlighted listings, homepage exposure)

Additional revenue streams:

Advertising placements (after sufficient traffic is reached)
Service offerings for businesses (e.g., website creation, digital marketing support, SEO)
Event promotion and ticketing commissions for Persian community events

The primary goal in the beginning is growth and trust, not immediate monetization.

2. Who adds the businesses?

The platform will follow a hybrid approach:

Initial phase: Businesses are added manually by the platform (supported by AI tools) to ensure quality and completeness
Growth phase: Business owners can submit their listings via a dedicated form
All submissions are reviewed and approved manually to maintain consistency and trust

This ensures both high-quality data and scalability over time.

3. What makes a business “verified” or “featured”?
Verified businesses:
Listings are manually checked to confirm that the business exists, is active, and has accurate contact information (e.g., website, address, social media)
Featured businesses (future):
Paid placements that provide increased visibility, such as:
Priority positioning in search results
Highlighted appearance on the platform
Inclusion in newsletters or promotions

Verification is based on trust and accuracy, while featuring is based on promotion.

4. Languages

The platform will be available in:

Persian (Farsi)
English

Users can switch languages easily, and the full interface will adapt accordingly.

Additional languages (such as German or French) may be considered in the future, but are not part of the initial scope.

5. What’s the most important action for a visitor?

The primary goal for users is to:

Quickly find relevant Persian businesses and community information in Switzerland

Key actions include:

Searching for businesses by category or location
Viewing results on an interactive map
Accessing contact details and external links (website, Google, Instagram)

The platform is designed for fast discovery, not interaction within the site.

6. Do you want reviews/ratings?

No.

The platform will not include its own review or rating system.
Instead, users will be directed to external platforms (such as Google) for reviews.

This keeps the platform:

Simple to manage
Free from moderation challenges
Focused on discovery rather than evaluation
7. Blog / News Section

Yes — a blog is a core part of the platform.

Content will include:

Guides for Persian speakers living in Switzerland (e.g., laws, migration, insurance, education)
Articles for newcomers and expats
Updates on Persian community events
Cultural and practical information

The blog also serves as a long-term growth channel (SEO) and helps establish trust and authority.

8. Target Audience

The platform targets two main groups:

Primary audience:
Persian (Farsi-speaking) individuals living in Switzerland
Newcomers and immigrants seeking trusted information and services
Secondary audience:
Non-Persian users interested in Persian businesses, culture, or events

The platform is primarily designed around the needs and language of the Persian-speaking community.
